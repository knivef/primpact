The problem

Recurring payments in crypto have historically been a high drop-off zone for merchants. Customers abandoned at every step: complex wallet setup, failed transactions, and no native way to pay with local currency.

The result was lost revenue at the moment of peak intent, with merchants falling back to legacy rails or losing the customer entirely.

Friction points merchants faced
  • Customers without existing wallets couldn't complete payments at all
  • Multi-step onboarding caused drop-offs before the first transaction
  • No seamless fiat-to-crypto conversion at the point of checkout
  • Recurring billing had no reliable trigger mechanism for crypto-native flows
The PayRam onramp solution
01
Wallet creation in seconds
Creates or connects a customer wallet within the checkout session. No prior crypto knowledge required.
02
Fiat-to-stablecoin on the fly
Converts local currency to the required stablecoin or token at point of checkout. Customer pays in fiat.
03
One-session recurring auth
Authorizes recurring payment permissions in the same session as wallet creation. No return visit needed.
04
Real-time merchant settlement
Settles directly to the merchant wallet instantly. No reconciliation lag, no manual processing.
